Tips for Local Farmer's Market Shopping:
Take a leisurely stroll around to survey and see what is available before you start buying.
Make a plan but be flexible, wait to buy the best and freshest.
Think realistically how much time you have to cook in the coming week.
Take your recipes with you. Don't overbuy.
Arrive early for the best selection and freshest products.
Once you return home, put some good music on and have yourself a produce-prepping party - pull out the bowls, colanders, cutting boards, and salad spinners... have fun! Enjoy your fresh foods!
