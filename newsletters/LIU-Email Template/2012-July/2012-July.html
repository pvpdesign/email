A Word About Health Coaching...
Remember... Health coaching will occur whenever you, our families, need it but IT IS UP TO YOU to make the contact - via phone call or email. The health coach will still attempt to contact you as frequently as is possible, but it is up to each family to make the BEST use of this unique benefit of being a part of our program. Families who do this have shown to make significantly better progress! You are assigned your health coach at your initial individual visits with the team -- if you do no know who your health coach is, please call the office at 407-303-9200. Talk soon!

Choosing a healthy cereal: The first ingredient should be a whole grain. It should have
< 10 grams of sugar, > 3 grams of fiber and < 3 grams of protein per serving. You can always mix a healthy cereal with a sweeter one for the "picky eater".
