Physicians Spotlight

Please welcome our newest physician as FHMG continues to grow and broaden its medical presence in Central Florida.

Andrew Asbury, DO
TeamCare Health Center

Dr. Asbury, board certified in family medicine, joins Paulina Castro, DO at TeamCare Health Center to expand the family medicine services available to Universal Orlando team members and their eligible dependents. Dr. Asbury received his medical degree from Kansas City University of Medicine and Biosciences and completed a family medicine residency at Summa Akron City Hospital. Dr. Asbury also holds advanced certifications in Nexplanon implantation. He provides comprehensive family medicine care and has advanced training in osteopathic manipulations and joint injections. In addition to an extensive list of teaching experience, research conducted, professional memberships held, conferences attended, presentations given and publications printed, Dr. Asbury possesses an undeniable calling to serve those in need, as evidenced by his numerous medical mission trips. 407.355.0803

Penelope Pauley, MD
Pediatric Endocrinology, Diabetes & Metabolism

Dr. Pauley is a board-certified pediatrician with advanced fellowship training in pediatric endocrinology practice and research. She recently completed a pediatric endocrine research fellowship at the University of Connecticut in Farmington, which was an extension of her pediatric endocrine fellowship at Connecticut Children’s Medical Center in Hartford. As a physician at the cutting edge of endocrinology research capabilities and revolutionary discoveries in progressive techniques, Dr. Pauley was chosen to join Dr. Jorge Daaboul and Dr. Konda Reddy to enhance the diagnostic and treatment capabilities at Pediatric Endocrinology, Diabetes & Metabolism. Her dedication to the field of medicine is evidenced by the impressive amount of research conducted, grants awarded, articles published, presentations and lectures given, time volunteered and professional memberships held. 407.896.2901

Craig Yunk, MD
Family Medicine of Celebration

Dr. Yunk, family medicine physician, has been selected and expertly trained to lead Family Medicine of Celebration, a flagship family medicine and pediatric specialist concept new to FHMG. As the chief resident of the Florida Hospital Family Medicine Residency program at Centre for Family Medicine in Winter Park, Dr. Yunk received specialized training in pediatrics, sports medicine and dermatology. As the founder of this premier Celebration facility, Dr. Yunk will apply his experience from the distinguished teaching practice by continuing to provide access to the latest research and technology available—offering leading-edge diagnostics, treatments and therapies—ensuring patients of every stage of life have the care they need to be as healthy and happy as possible. 407.566.0404
 


Q2 Press Ganey Results Are In!

There are 46 practices scoring in the 75th percentile or higher overall based on the national Press Ganey survey report for Q2. Scoring in the 75th percentile or higher in patient satisfaction is one of FHMG’s goals for attaining our vision “To be the Preeminent Multi-Specialty Medical Group of Central Florida.” Six of these esteemed practices scored in the top 1% nationally with their overall patient experience.

The oncology practices showed tremendous improvement for their patient satisfaction scores for Q2. Katie Zacher, Director of Oncology Operations, had this to say about her team. “It takes very special, caring people to work in the Oncology environment and I am so proud of all of those who have made a continued effort to remember what each patient in front of them is going through, and the compassion that they would need if they were in that patient or loved one’s shoes. It isn’t always easy, but they care so much for their patients and it is showing”, says Katie.

The 2013 Employee Engagement Survey

We must need some rest, but we can't stop now! As of Friday, August 9, we are at 86% with 1117 employees who have participated since we started the Gallup race. The Survey ends Sunday, August 18, so please help us reach 100%!

Reminders:
  • Providers (Physicians and mid-levels do not participate in this survey)
  • You must be hired by June 5th to participate in the survey
  • Survey Dates: Sunday, July 28 – Sunday, August 18
  • Methods of Participation: IVR and Web Access
  • Survey Web Site: https://gx.gallup.com/adventistq12.gx
  • Survey Phone Number: 1.866.751.7127
  • Access Code is Six Digit Birth Date and Last Four Digits of SS# (e.g. 0522664408 - for birth date of May 22, 1966 and last four of social security number of 4408).
  • Our goal is 100% participation. The deadline for completing the survey is August 18, 2013 at 11:59 p.m. (EST).
Your feedback in the Employee Engagement Survey is highly valued and greatly appreciated. Thank you for your participation. After you have completed the survey, be sure to check with your manager to receive the I Am Florida Hospital Badge Buddy pin!
