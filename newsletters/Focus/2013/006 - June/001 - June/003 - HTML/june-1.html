Physicians Spotlight

Please welcome our newest physicians as FHMG continues to grow and broaden its medical presence in Central Florida.

Our newest FHMG practice, Central Florida Internal Medicine, led by Tanya C. Haber, DO, FACOI and Erdem M. Aydur, MD, opens on June 3, 2013. With a strong passion for osteopathic medicine and treating the patient holistically, the team provides comprehensive, internal medicine services for patients age 18 and older. As established physicians with a strong affiliation to Florida Hospital, patients will have access to a more comprehensive continuum of care. The practice is located at 401 North Mills Avenue in Suite C, with convenient access to I-4. 407.893.6225

Tanya C. Haber, DO, FACOI
Central Florida Internal Medicine

Dr. Haber is board certified in osteopathic internal medicine and has completed advanced osteopathic medical examiners training. Beginning in 2001, she has honed internal medicine skills, including chronic disease management, acute illness care, women’s and men’s health, urgent care and preventive medicine for patients 18 and older. Since 2007, Dr. Haber has provided traditional hospitalist services in greater Orlando. Dr. Haber has served as the program director of internal medicine residency program at several university hospitals, a clinical instructor, staff physician and assistant professor.

Erdem M. Aydur, MD
Central Florida Internal Medicine

Dr. Aydur is board certified in internal medicine and has provided primary care medical services and expertise to peers and patients since 1992. He provides comprehensive internal medicine skills, including chronic disease management, acute illness care, men’s and women’s health care, urgent care, immunizations and preventive medicine. Previous positions held include clinical assistant professor for medical students, and staff physician, senior physician and chief health officer for the Florida Department of Corrections.
 


Physician Awards from Vitals 2013

Consumer choice is becoming an increasingly important factor in patient decision making. Many patients now go online to Vitals or Health Grades to read comments left about the physicians before making a decision whether to go to that doctor. To that end, we would like to share some FHMG physicians who received special acknowledgement on Vitals.

Vitals (vitals.com), a leading online physician information and ratings resource, helps patients easily identify the highest-rated doctors in their neighborhoods and honors local physicians who’ve received the highest praise from their patients online. Accolades such as the Patient’s Choice Award and Compassion Award are chosen by patients who visit Vitals in search of a new doctor, totaling 120 million people each year. Vitals analyzes these patient-provided ratings in order to identify those physicians within a certain specialty and neighborhood who are consistently rated highly.

The following physicians have earned the Patient's Choice Award from Vitals and are highly regarded by their patients for consistently providing exceptional care. It’s important to note that only 5% of all doctors in the United States are recognized with this award.

Our 2nd Quarter Mission: The Russell Home

Since 1946, The Russell Home has served as a non-profit institute and home for brain-damaged children, the first of its kind in the country. It is a true “home” where children whose handicap prevents them from living independently, are provided specialized care with lots of love. There are currently 26 children living in the home and they receive no government funding.
