Exploring MyAccess

We are excited to introduce to you MyAccess, an innovative concept for patients to access their health information in a secure environment and at their convenience. MyAccess will be important to our patients but also will provide some operational benefits for our employees:

  1. Convenient, secure access. Patients will now be able to access their information quickly and securely without the involvement of any office staff.
  2. Reduction of phone calls to the office. By enabling the patient to access their information independently, this will reduce record request phone calls received by the practice. The amount of this reduction will be directly tied to the number of patients enrolled in MyAccess.
  3. Journey towards preeminence. Providing our patients this access to their health information is aligned with our mission. This functionality will eventually be a standard component to patient care is required for future meaningful use attestations.
To get started, provide us with your email address and within 24 hours— you will receive an email from Florida Hospital MyAccess with directions on how to create your own MyAccess account and password. If you have any questions regarding MyAccess, please contact AccountHelp@FLHosp.org.
