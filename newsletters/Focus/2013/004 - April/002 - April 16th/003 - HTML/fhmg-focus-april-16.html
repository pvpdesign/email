Dr. Keith Kim Performs 1000th Robotic Surgery

Keith Kim, general surgeon at Advanced Minimally Invasive & Bariatric Surgical Consultants, completed his 1,000th robotic case on April 16, 2013. Dr. Kim is the first bariatric surgeon in the world to achieve 1,000 robotic cases using the da Vinci Surgical System. To celebrate this milestone, Florida Hospital, along with Intuitive Surgical, held a special event in Dr. Kim’s honor for his accomplishments. Executives from Intuitive’s corporate office in California attended via Skype video conference to acknowledge Dr. Kim’s achievements and present him with a plaque. Please help us congratulate Dr. Kim and his bariatric team for this momentous success!

The Russell Home: Our 2nd Quarter Mission

Since 1946, The Russell Home has served as a non-profit institute and home for brain-damaged children, the first of its kind in the country. It is a true “home” where children whose handicap prevents them from living independently, are provided specialized care with lots of love. There are currently 26 children living in the home and they receive no government funding.
