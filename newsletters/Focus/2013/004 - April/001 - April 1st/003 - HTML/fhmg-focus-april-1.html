April 1, 2013

The Patient Portal is Open

Portal at a Glance
The new Patient Portal allows patients who seek treatment at any Florida Hospital to enroll during patient registration. Once this option is selected, the patient receives an invitation by email to subscribe. As a subscribed member, the patient will be able to view their online health summary, such as known allergies, medications, problems and lab results on file in Cerner.
**Doctor office notes are not part of the Health Summary that can be accessed by patients.

What's My Role?
Beginning April 1, 2013, we encourage you to invite your patients to register for the Patient Portal. There is a new feature in the Cerner system for you to enter a patient’s email during your registration conversation. Once a patient is registered, an invitation will be sent to the provided email address with specific enrollment instructions.

For patients who choose not to provide an email address at the time of their visit, but call back later and decide they would like to register for the Patient Portal, please follow the steps below.
  1. Open the Conversation Launcher
  2. Select “FHMG/Fri/FH Add/Modify Person” conversation
  3. Search for your patient
  4. Once you select the appropriate patient and the conversation opens, change the online access to “Yes” and enter their email address
  5. Save your changes when exiting the conversation
