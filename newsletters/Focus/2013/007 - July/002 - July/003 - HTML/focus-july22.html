Physicians Spotlight

Please welcome our newest physician as FHMG continues to grow and broaden its medical presence in Central Florida.

George J. Nassif, Jr. DO
Center for Colon & Rectal Surgery

Upon completing his fellowship training at Center for Colon & Rectal Surgery, Dr. Nassif has joined Dr. Matthew Albert in the Altamonte Springs office. Dr. Nassif pursued general surgery residency training at Allegheny General Hospital in Pittsburgh, as well as a minimally invasive laparoscopic colorectal cancer fellowship at The Lankenau Medical Center in Wynnwood, PA. As the newest member of the colorectal team who provided his advanced training, Dr. Nassif is exceptionally equipped to provide comprehensive treatments and minimally-invasive surgery for a multitude of colorectal disorders, conditions and cancers, including pelvic 3D endorectal ultrasound, TAMIS (transanal minimally invasive surgery), TEM (transanal endoscopic microsurgery) and SILS (single incision laparoscopic surgery). 407.303.5191
 


Get Ready to Gallup!

The annual Employee Engagement survey will be starting next week. Please see this important message from Mark Martin, our Chief Operating Officer, and information from Gallup about this upcoming survey. Your opinion counts!

Thank you for the contribution you provide to Florida Hospital Medical Group (FHMG), and the role you play in helping our organization deliver excellent health care. Movin' on Up at FHMG



As a leading employer in Central Florida, FHMG strives to provide an atmosphere of encouragement and empowerment for all employees. One of the ways we do this is by creating strong avenues for professional development across each and every department. We promote growth from within, affording all staff the opportunity to pursue their desired career goals.

To meet eligibility requirements, employees must be in their current position for six months and must not currently be subject to formal discipline before seeking a transfer to another department. You can search for open positions within FHMG by going to HR Online, logging in using your FHMG username and password, and selecting openings where you work. You can also visit FHMedicalGroup.com (from any location without having to be connected to the intranet) for a list of job openings. It is highly recommended that you notify your manager of your interest to transfer.

From the intranet you can utilize Net Learning for professional development. There are many subject matters available including Energy for Performance, Spiritual Ambassador, Microsoft and Clinical. Subject matters may be class time or CBL (computer based learning).

To stay up to date on the latest career opportunities at FHMG, please browse the job postings on our corporate website.
 


Backpacks for Kids



Our FHMG family once again has the opportunity to give students a great start to the school year. The Backpack for Kids program is designed to help underprivileged children who are not able to buy a new backpack and school supplies for their first day of school. Last year, FHMG employees donated 105 backpacks! To help us surpass last year’s total, please see the donation tips below.

Backpack Donation Quick Tips
  • Fill backpacks with Kindergarten through 5th grade supplies
  • Please send the backpacks and supplies by courier to Stephanie Whitley at the Maitland corporate office.
  • Mark everything BACKPACK MISSION and Budget Courier will deliver it at no charge
  • BE SURE your practice/department name is clearly marked on each item
  • All backpacks and supplies must be delivered by August 7 which is pickup day
Please remember we accept empty backpacks, miscellaneous supplies from the list and filled backpacks for any grade level. Please use the grade level tags which are included in the links below. Also, FHMG employees who may not be able to provide a backpack and supplies for one or more of their children (K-5th grade) may fill out the attached application and send it to Stephanie Whitley along with their latest paycheck.

The annual Florida Hospital Backpacks for Kids campaign will run Monday, July 22 through Wednesday, August 7. Please deliver all backpacks (and any FHMG employee applications) to Stephanie Whitley at the Trickel Building in Maitland for delivery to Florida Hospital and distribution. Please be sure not to send anything to Stephanie Jones at Florida Hospital.

Thanks for making a difference in the life of a child! For more information about donating or to request an application, please contact Stephanie.Whitley@flhosp.org.
