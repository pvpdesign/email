Children’s Center for Cancer and CIF East Orlando Receive Award

The Children's Center for Cancer & Blood Diseases and Cancer Institute of Florida East Orlando were both recognized for achieving a level of excellence by COLA. Children’s Center for Cancer received the award for their
standard of excellence in administering blood transfusions to children.

Physicians Spotlight

Please welcome our newest physicians as FHMG continues to grow and broaden its medical presence in Central Florida.

WILLIAM D. FINLAYSON, III, MD
LAKE INTERNAL MEDICINE

Dr. Finlayson is board certified in internal medicine and is the sole physician at Lake Internal Medicine. Since 1981, he has provided exceptional medical care with a focus on acute and chronic disease management and cardiopulmonary health for which he performs EKGs and pulmonary function tests directly in his Eustis, Florida practice. He has held many prestigious positions at Florida Hospital Waterman, including Director of the Cardiopulmonary Department and Chairman of both the Department of Medicine and Pharmacy and Therapeutics at Waterman. 352.589.6367

YVONNE M. BUCHANAN, MD
LAKESIDE INTERNAL MEDICINE ASSOCIATES

Dr. Buchanan is board certified in internal medicine and joins Dr. Fernandez and Dr. Unyon at Lakeside Internal Medicine Associates. For the past 30 years, she has provided excellent medical care to residents of Sanford, Mount Dora and the surrounding communities. Dr. Buchanan has held many prestigious positions at Florida Hospital, including Physician Advisor Case Management/Chairperson and Chief of Department of Medicine. She has also held the position of Chief of the Department of Medicine at Central Florida Regional Hospital. 352.343.5722

KASHIF QURESHI, MD
LONGWOOD FAMILY HEALTH

Dr. Qureshi is board certified in family practice and joins Dr. Cheryl Oh at Longwood Family Health. He completed his residency training at Florida Hospital in Winter Park, Florida. Since 1995, the medical team at Longwood Family Health has offered comprehensive primary care services to residents of Altamonte Springs and the surrounding communities. Both Dr. Oh and Dr. Qureshi have a special interest in preventive care and office-based dermatologic procedures, such as cryosurgery, skin biopsies and skin cancer screenings. 407.862.3400

FHMG Leadership Spotlight

As you may remember, we’ve been highlighting FHMG leadership in an effort to familiarize everyone with our executive team. As we move into 2013, here’s the last, but certainly not least, of our leadership team.

SCOTT HILL
VP OF ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICES

Scott Hill joined FHMG in January of 2011 as Vice President of Administrative Services. Scott has over 20 years of experience in physician practice management, most recently serving as the Chief Operating Officer for After Hours Pediatrics where he was responsible for 12 pediatric urgent care clinics in Orlando, Tampa and South Florida. Scott received a BS degree in Health Administration from Auburn University and a Masters in Health Administration from Medical College of Virginia in Richmond. In his moments away from the office, Scott enjoys spending time with his wife Jennifer and their two sons, Ryan and Jarrett.
