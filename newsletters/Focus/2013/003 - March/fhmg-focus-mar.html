Week of March 4, 2013

Dr. Gomez Performs First Pediatric Robotic Surgery in Latin America

Leading a team from Florida Hospital, Dr. Pablo Gomez III, fellowship-trained physician at Florida Center for Pediatric Urology and Medical Director of the Pediatric Urology Robotic Program at Florida Hospital for Children, recently performed the first pediatric robotic surgery in Latin America. Dr. Gomez, a third-generation physician from Colombia, performed the procedure on a child with a congenital defect at Clinica Marly in Bogota, Colombia. In addition to the surgery, Dr. Gomez and leaders from Florida Hospital visited with key Colombian health officials to discuss potential shared opportunities in advancing health care in the region.

Advanced Surgical Care Specialists

The elite and established surgical team at Advanced Surgical Care Specialists, formerly General and Vascular Surgical Associates, PA, joined FHMG in February 2013. Located in Lake Mary, the practice offers the latest in surgical techniques, including open, endoscopic, and minimally-invasive laparoscopic and robotic procedures. The surgery center is led by John W. Robertson, MD, FACS, William L. Huether, III, MD, FACS and Nicole BaiRossi, MD, FACS. All three surgeons are board certified and trained in robotic and single-incision surgery (robotic single port surgery), to provide patients with expert, compassionate care for a broad range of surgical needs. 407.833.9195

Leyberth Rosendo, MD
Mid-Florida Family Medicine

Dr. Rosendo is board certified in family medicine and has been practicing primary care medicine since 1995, with nearly 15 years in Central Florida. Since opening his Apopka-based practice in 1999, Dr. Rosendo has provided comprehensive family medical care to patients five years and older, including occupational medicine and urgent care. In addition to special interests in managing chronic illness and sleep medicine, he can perform many lab tests, screenings, immunizations and procedures right in his office, such as EKG and Holter monitoring, digital X-rays, ultrasounds, urinalysis, spirometry and much more. The practice provides appointments as early as 6:30 am on Wednesday and Friday. Dr. Rosendo is fluent in English and Spanish. 407.889.8008
